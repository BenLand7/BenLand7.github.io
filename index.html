About Me

I am a student at Juniata College in Huntingdon, Pennsilvania. I am majoring in Integrated Media Arts. I am also a player on the Juniata Mens Basketball Team. I work with the Juniata Sports information department and create graphics for their instagram page. I am also a trainer / coach at Redline Athletics located in Kensignton, MD.

I have always been interested in arts but I started becoming interested in animation and digital media as a medium in highschool. since then I have experimented in all forms of digital art, specifically creating graphics in photoshop or animating/editing in Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro.
